Our Mission

Exchange skills, not money

We started SkillSwap because we noticed something broken about how people learn. Courses are expensive. Tutors are expensive. But knowledge itself? That's everywhere — in the people around you.

SkillSwap connects people who want to teach with people who want to learn, and lets them trade skills directly. You teach me guitar, I teach you Python. Simple, fair, and free.

"I can code. You can paint. We both want to learn."

What We Believe

Our values

🌍
Knowledge is for everyone
Learning shouldn't be gated behind paywalls. Everyone deserves access to skills regardless of their financial situation.
🔄
Give and take equally
Every swap is a two-way exchange. No one is just a student or just a teacher — everyone plays both roles.
🤝
Trust is everything
Reviews, ratings, and transparent profiles keep our community honest, safe, and high quality for everyone.
🌱
Community over competition
A rises B rises C. When one person grows, they lift others. We build together, not against each other.
